Fred The Baker Baking Co.

Fred The Baker Baking Company© has been in service for over 50 years, and features the infamous 'Mushpuppy Donut', if you haven't tried one you are really missing out! As part of this long tenured history of service to the North East Ohio area, Fred has invited you all to partake in this fun game to really send him into retirement with a bang, as well as keep his donut-making alive long after hes retired.

Cookie Clicker est. 2020

The inspiration for Donut maker comes from a game started in 2020 called Cookie Clicker©! In Cookie Clicker, users are able to click to earn cookies, with rewards for a certain amount of clicks as well as all types of bells and whistles to make the game enjoyable for the user. Therefore, in rememberance of Cookie Clicker the 2021 Spring Cohort presents to you Donut-Maker©
